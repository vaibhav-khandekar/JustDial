This collection is our kindest and purest formulation yet, with no compromise on colour, quality or wear time. Enjoy this toxin-free formulation which will give you a full coverage, chip-free, ultra-glossy & long-lasting manicure.

Color
Pink

How to use?

Dry Manicure (cut, file, shape). Apply Base Coat, allow it to dry. Apply the first coat of colour, allow it to dry. Apply the second coat of colour and allow it to dry. Complete your manicure with a Gloss or Matte Top coat for extra shine & volume.

 Features

Toxin free nail polish. Free from Toluene, Dibutyl Pthalate, Formaldehyde, Formaldehyde Resin, Camphor, Ethyl Tossylamide, Tri Phenyl Phosphate, Lead, Xylene.

Easy & mess-free application with our signature round cut brush.Long lasting colour in 2 coats. Goes on easily and dries quickly.

Does not leave yellow stains on the nail bed. Helps maintains healthy & strong nails.

 Shelf Life

Consume within 12 months of opening.

 Ingredients

Nitrocellulose, Polyester Resin Acetyl tri butyl citrate, Ethyl Acetate, Butyl Acetate, Iso-Propyl Alcohol, n-Butyl Alcohol, Phenaxy Methanol Acetate, Acrylate Co Polymer, Steary Iconium, Hectorite.

Car Detailing Services in Chembur, Mumbai

 

Paint Protection Film in Chembur, Mumbai

UltrashieldX paint protection film is crafted from premium thermoplastic urethane. This PPF, available in transparent or various colours, is designed to coat the uppermost paint layer of cars and bikes. Its key feature is the self-healing property, which actively guards against damage such as chips, scratches, and marks caused by rocks, sand, and other similar elements, ensuring the vehicle maintains a youthful appearance.
 

Ceramic Coating in Chembur, Mumbai

Ceramic coatings offer robust resistance against chemicals and provide superior UV protection to hinder paint oxidation. Often referred to as nano-coatings or glass coatings, they not only safeguard your vehicle but also enhance the shine and lustre of the car's body.
 

Car/Bike Detailing in Chembur, Mumbai

Professional detailing embodies the skilful practice of meticulously cleaning and refurbishing a vehicle to return it to its pristine, original state. This process is far more detailed and requires greater labour than a standard car wash, offering your vehicle a comprehensive transformation.
 

Ceramic Wash in Chembur, Mumbai

A blend of siloxanes that bonds with the car's exterior, offering protection, flexibility (capacity to bend), and superior foaming and lubrication during cleaning. This compound integrates with your car’s paint, creating an extra layer that is hydrophobic for added protection.
 

Steam Wash in Chembur, Mumbai

The steam car wash is an environmentally conscious method that utilizes steam for cleaning both the interior and exterior of a vehicle. This green approach effectively eliminates unpleasant odours, bacteria, and germs from the car, making it a favoured eco-friendly cleaning technique.
 

Maintenance Wash in Chembur, Mumbai

The term 'maintenance car wash' is widely recognized in the detailing industry. Various methods exist for this procedure, but routine cleaning is often regarded as the optimal approach. Regular maintenance not only extends the lifespan of your vehicle but also ensures it looks consistently new and well-kept.
 

Window Film in Chembur, Mumbai

UltrashieldX window films are proven to obstruct up to 97% of infrared rays, effectively eliminating concerns about harmful solar radiation re-entering the vehicle. By combining Nano-ceramic materials with metalized coatings, this innovative hybrid technology revolutionizes the concept of delicate endotherms, introducing a new class of hybrid ceramic window films.
 

Car Sanitization in Chembur, Mumbai

Vehicle sanitization involves the use of ozone treatment, where ozone (O3) gas effectively eliminates odours, bacteria, and viruses present in the car. As the gas permeates the vehicle's interior, it neutralizes unpleasant smells and eradicates harmful bacteria and viruses.
